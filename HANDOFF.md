The build phase is done. On-page is strong; from here, growth is a function of **distribution and time**, not more pages. This is your one-page orientation: what to do, in what order, and which doc covers each part.

The bottleneck now is **authority (links) + indexing + time** — nothing more to build to remove it.

## Do these first (in order)

1. **Deploy this build.** Unzip over a clean checkout, then commit and push:
   `unzip -o taxpreparertools-seo-update.zip` → `git add -A` → `git commit -m "Reference pages, EITC calc, embed widgets, en-US"` → `git push`
2. **Index it.** Google Search Console → URL Inspection → "Request indexing" for the new pages (the six reference pages, `/eitc-calculator`, `/embed`); resubmit `sitemap.xml`. Then add the site to **Bing Webmaster Tools** (import from GSC) and submit the sitemap. *(10 minutes; do it the same day you deploy.)*
3. **Start embed outreach** — your single highest-ROI link source. 10-15 personalized emails a week to finance/gig/CPA blogs. Template + targets in `DISTRIBUTION-KIT.md` §1.
4. **Submit to directories** — AlternativeTo, SaaSHub, and the rest. Copy/paste descriptions in `DISTRIBUTION-KIT.md` §2.
5. **Launch on Product Hunt** — taglines, description, maker's comment, and launch mechanics are ready in `DISTRIBUTION-KIT.md` §3.
6. **Seed communities, rules-first** — value-first posts/comments for r/tax, r/taxpros, and CPA groups in `DISTRIBUTION-KIT.md` §4.

## What "working" looks like (track monthly in GSC)

- **Impressions** rising for Tier-1 queries (transcript/notice/reject codes, specific 2026 figures) = pages are indexed and showing.
- **Average position** moving toward page one — anything at position 8-20 is a few links away.
- **Referring domains** climbing as embeds and directory links land — the metric most tied to reach.

Judge progress by these three, never by your rank for the head term.
