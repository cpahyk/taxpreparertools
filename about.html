Our Mission

Why TaxPreparerTools Exists

Tax season puts CPAs, Enrolled Agents, and preparers under extreme pressure. The tools most professionals rely on are either buried inside expensive software subscriptions, too slow to use during client calls, or simply don't exist in standalone form.

TaxPreparerTools was built to change that. Every calculator on this platform reflects current IRS guidance — 2025 and 2026 figures, updated for inflation adjustments and legislative changes. Our tools are designed to produce a result in under 60 seconds, without requiring a login or a credit card.

We believe the fundamentals of tax calculation should be freely accessible to every professional and every taxpayer. The complex software can handle the filing — we handle the understanding.

Editorial Standards

How We Keep Accuracy

Every tool and resource on TaxPreparerTools is grounded in primary IRS sources: Revenue Procedures, Revenue Rulings, IRS Publications, and the Internal Revenue Code itself. When Congress passes new legislation or the IRS issues revised guidance, we update our tools — typically within 48 hours of an official IRS announcement.

Our editorial process includes:

  • Annual review of all calculators at the start of each tax year
  • Cross-referencing IRS Publication updates (Pub 946 for depreciation, Pub 463 for travel/mileage, Pub 535 for business expenses)
  • Validation of bracket and threshold figures against official IRS revenue procedures
  • Review of relevant tax court decisions and IRS FAQ updates
